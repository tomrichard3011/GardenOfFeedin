Welcome to the Garden of Feedin'!

Our mission is to connect the community with their local foodbanks and non-profit organizations who will help distribute food to those in need. With food insecurity at an all-time high with the onset of the pandemic, we want to allow an easy solution to bring fresh produce from garden to plate.

For Growers

Easy-to-use site

With a simple and intuitive design, our site is catered to provide the best user experience. Our site is easy to navigate through and provides users with features all with a click of a button. Sign-up today for a fast and easy way to donate those extra fruits and vegetables!

Rewards System & Community Building

Keep track of what you donate through the site. Get special, limited-edition badges for donating to display on your profile and get recognized by your community. Stay up-to-date with your local area through a news feed specialized for you.

Just list your extra food

... and we will notify you of local food banks and organizations that would like to collect your home-grown produce. Add your time availability to ensure a smoother pick-up.

For Organizations

Sourced locally

Your business will be notified of donors in the region that have the produce your are looking for.

Track donations

Type of donations and quantity of donations can be tracked through our site. Certain logistics and statistics are also available.

Request specific produce

Business can post their preferred produce on their organization profile for gardeners to see. Sets consisting of more than one food item can also be created for bulk donations.
